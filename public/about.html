About Me

K Jyoshna Alekhya is from Kothagudem, a picturesque town in Telangana known for its rich cultural and historical significance. Growing up in this vibrant region, Jyoshna developed a strong academic foundation and a keen interest in technology. Her journey led her to Sathyabhama Institute of Technology, where she pursued a degree in Computer Science Engineering. This period was instrumental in shaping her technical skills and expanding her knowledge in the field of computing.

During her academic career, Jyoshna gained practical experience through a six-month internship at Arcon Tech Solutions, where she worked as a Business Analyst. This role allowed her to bridge the gap between technical solutions and business needs, enhancing her understanding of both technical and strategic aspects of technology. The internship was a significant milestone, providing her with valuable insights and hands-on experience in a professional setting.

Outside of her professional pursuits, Jyoshna has a deep passion for reading fantasy books, which fuels her creativity and imagination. Looking ahead, she is excited about developing a career as a web developer. With a strong technical background and a commitment to continuous learning, Jyoshna is well-positioned to make meaningful contributions to the field of web development and achieve her career aspirations.
